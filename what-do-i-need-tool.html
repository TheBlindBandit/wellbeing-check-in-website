Before we begin

How settled do you feel right now?

No right answer — this helps us start in the right place.

Your nervous system may need regulating before resting.

When the system is highly activated, it can't receive rest until it begins to downshift. Try this first:

  1. Physiological sigh: Double inhale through the nose (breathe in, then top up), then one long slow exhale through the mouth. Repeat 3 times.
  2. 5-4-3-2-1 grounding: 5 things you can see. 4 you can touch. 3 you can hear. 2 you can smell. 1 you can taste.
  3. Then continue to find your rest type below.
